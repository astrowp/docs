# How to Use the Yoast SEO Plugin

The Yoast SEO plugin helps optimize your content for search engines like Google, increasing your chances of achieving better rankings. This guide will walk you through using the Yoast SEO meta box for posts and pages.

### Accessing the Yoast SEO Meta Box

You will find the meta box at the bottom of the page editor:

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/38c9e4d0-3966-46e6-9188-e57b706a1f3e" width="750px" />

If you are using the block editor, click the Yoast icon at the top right to access the Yoast SEO meta box:

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/9f9bf837-a7b0-41ea-bb58-bbc45fffec67" width="750px" />

### SEO Tab

The SEO tab analyzes your post’s content in real-time using a traffic light system to rate your content as good, okay, or needs improvement.

#### Focus Keyphrase

A focus keyphrase (or keyword) is the main word or phrase you want your content to rank for in search results.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/5c0f9bfb-9b46-4cf8-8aad-a7f61341ae36" width="750px" />

Click the **Get related keyphrases** button in the meta box to add more related keywords you wish to rank for.

#### Google Preview

Google Preview shows how your post will appear in search results for both desktop and mobile formats.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/93df72d9-53bb-47cb-8dfb-c07d1f9f6842" width="750px" />

#### SEO Title & Slug (URL)

Optimize the SEO Page Title and the URL slug for the best results. Ensure both are concise and include your keyphrase.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/ad85016a-8172-4a48-b26c-ad35feba9108" width="750px" />

#### Meta Description

The meta description serves as a "free" ad in search results. Include your keyword(s) and accurately describe the content to entice potential visitors to click.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/1d5a0672-ff90-4f2d-8c88-52c67deb2f88" width="750px" />

Yoast allows you to insert variables into the meta description, such as site title, post title, primary category, and separator.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/1fad2ef8-bced-4038-ab5f-b99b7d509bd6" width="750px" />

#### SEO Analysis

Yoast provides an on-page SEO score and a checklist for optimization. It identifies problems, areas for improvement, and good results.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/43734f9f-7c55-45fe-b260-1d48e0db0b26" width="750px" />

For best results, write quality content from the start. For tips, check out how to write quality SEO content.

The SEO analysis also checks if you have images with keyphrases in their alt text.

#### Cornerstone Content

Mark important posts as cornerstone content here.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/f2faf5f6-c956-4b81-b096-c8fbb8968e82" width="750px" />

#### Advanced Features

In the Advanced section, you have additional SEO options for your post. You can control whether search engines show the post in search results, set follow links, and select robot options.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/f1b91b99-5eb5-4348-8756-eb993a0c2a28" width="750px" />

#### Insights

View the Flesch reading ease score, word count, and estimated reading time of the post.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/a2b6a060-7865-40a3-b5cc-d7965bd85c51" width="750px" />

### Readability Tab

Yoast's proprietary algorithm provides reliable readability results. Aim for a readability score of 8, as most people are comfortable reading at this level.

Like SEO analysis, scores are color-coded: red (problems), orange (improvements), and green (good results). Clicking the “eye” icon next to each item highlights problem areas.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/d50c8d3e-7287-47d7-bab9-a6f88d869739" width="750px" />

### Schema Tab

Use Schema markup to optimize your site’s SEO. You can change schema settings in the dashboard or on individual posts and pages.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/6479a358-4106-49b4-9f92-43b47e71df35" width="750px" />

### Social Tab

Control how your post’s preview is displayed on social media platforms. Assign a custom image, title, and description for each post or page.

<img src="https://github.com/astrowp/docs/assets/170225022/8a7048a7-a64f-4835-ab39-a316ed364129" width="750px" />
